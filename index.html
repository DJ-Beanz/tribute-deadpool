Deadpool (Wade Wilson)

The Merc with a Mouth: breaking fourth walls and villains’ plans since forever-ish

Why Deadpool deserves a tribute (besides the katanas)

Wade Wilson, a.k.a. Deadpool, is a regenerating degenerate with a healing factor, dual katanas, and the uncanny ability to know he’s in a web page. He’s chaotic good… on days ending with “y.” Despite the snark and snack obsessions, he occasionally saves the day between bites of chimichangas.

Highlights (totally unbiased)

  • Breaks the fourth wall so often it’s basically a sliding door.
  • Healing factor: paper cuts fear him; explosions annoy him.
  • Katanas sharper than his jokes (debatable).
  • Team-ups range from X-Force to “whoever’s nearby and not already running.”
  • PhD in Maximum Effort; minor in Poor Life Choices.
“Life is an endless series of train-wrecks with only brief, commercial-like breaks of happiness.” — Deadpool
